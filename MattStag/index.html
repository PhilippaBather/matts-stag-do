Friday

Friday

Keeping it Session

17:00 Ibis Albert Dock, Liverpool

Established as a brothel-cum boathouse in 1846 this up-market hotel now tends to weary travellers looking for that little bit of je ne sais quois. The hotel is well situated and benefits from parking. That said, Kath's review would have it otherwise:

Although the hotel is well situated, and easy to find and park I would never stay there again unless there is a change of staff. We wanted to show our family from Canada the home of the Beatles but I was embarrassed by the service we received at this Ibis." Kath261 - Oct. 2017 - 2 stars TripAdvisor

Parking

Arriving by car and looking for a place to park your new Rover 75?...

Parking at the hotel is £10 per day on a first come first serve basis. Alternative parking at Q Park; a 4 minute walk away at just shy of £20 a day (at time of writing).

19:00 Food & Drink

Almost Famous American burger joint based in the north of England followed by beers at various watering holes.

**Alert friends to the dangers of ales above 4.5% ABV wilst trying to 'Keep it Session'.**

Saturday Day

Saturday Day

Catton Halls Stag Experience

An action packed day of typical 'manly' activities. Stag, stag, stag...lads, lads, lads...only our stag is into golf and his Jag (think Partridge shy of 30). So, no piss up before 12:00, rather all stags to arrive at 11:30 at Catton Hall situated in the beautiful Cheshire countryside. Boasting 200 acres of land, the estate is self-described as the 'Premier Outdoor Activitiy Venue' and 'The place for serious fun!'. Naturally, all activities to be undertaken will gesture to masculine pretensions:

  • 12:00 Air Riffle Experience
  • 13:00 Lunch at the Shooting Lodge
  • 14:00 Ultimate 9 Miles Quad Trek

Note: no specific clothing required - helmets, overalls, balaclavas will be provided; no prizes for being any good. And, Matt and Alex: bring shoes you're happy to get covered in mud...not your favourite Nike Air Vapour Max.

Sunday

Sunday's Shame Well

And it all hits home. The highs, the hangover, and feeling of unconscionable guilt at nothing and everything in particular. The hotel's fry up will sort it out one way or another. That and confession. Don't forget that Liverpool is home to the Grade 2 Listed Metropolitan Catholic Cathedral locally nicknamed "Paddy's Wigwam" for it's circular design. Remember Catholicism and guilt go hand in hand.

  • 11:00 Catholic Catherdral ALL SINS ABSOLVED (optional)
  • 13:00 Food at Allerton Manor Golf Club

Finally, head home, regroup and back for more on 4th September 2021 for the wedding of the year.
